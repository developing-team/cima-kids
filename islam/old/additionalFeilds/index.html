FAQs

How do I verify my email?
Click the link in the verification email from verify@moviekids.com (be sure to check your spam folder or other email tabs if it's not in your inbox). Or, send an email with the subject "Verify" to verify@moviekids.com from the email address you use for your account.

My movie is buffering or not loading.
It's likely an issue with your internet connection or the movie server. To fix, check your internet connection or try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, contact our support team for assistance.

How do I contact the creator of a movie?
You can leave a comment on any public movie. Click the "Comments" link in the movie details view, or visit the movie's page.

What version of [library] does Movie Kids use?
We have our current list of library versions
What are favorites?
A favorite is a movie that you can save to your own account and watch later. Your favorite list includes all the movies you have saved and you can watch them anytime.
